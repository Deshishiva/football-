Goal Shock Trading Engine

Submission for Omniverse Fund – AI/ML / Quant Intern assessment. This page gives a quick overview of the project and links to the code.

1. Project Summary

This engine listens to real-time football match events and reacts when an underdog team scores a goal. It attempts to exploit short-term price lag on prediction markets by placing fast buy orders before the market adjusts.

2. What the System Does

  • Receives goal updates (mock feed for testing)
  • Normalizes incoming events
  • Maps events to synthetic markets
  • Simulates market prices
  • Trader decides "BUY" when underdog scores
  • Logs all decisions
